Hi, I’m Garbo — your personal email cleanup assistant.

I was created to help you quickly delete unwanted emails and save you the time and stress of sorting through hundreds — even thousands — of messages from a sender. "Email hoarding" has become a big thing for many humans, and I am here to alleviate the stress of sorting and removing these large sums of emails from your email's database.

Here is how I work:

If you are not a member yet create an account within my system, and once it is validated through the email address that you would like to give me permission to have access within then I can begin my work.

  1. Using the search bar near the top of the page enter keywords related to a sender then I will search through your emails that have the keyword within their messages. You may also directly type in the email address of the sender(s) which will have a more accurate and faster approach.
    • If multiple emails will be used in the search bar be sure to separate them with a comma ","
  2. I will display all the emails from the sender(s), which will be sorted from the most recent month and year.
    • These emails will all be selected and ready for immediate deletion
    • If there is an email or more that you would like not selected for deletion simply unselect it and it will not be deleted
  3. I will highlight important emails — like bills — to help ensure nothing critical gets deleted.
  4. When you're ready to delete, I’ll guide you through two safety steps:
    • A confirmation prompt asking if you're sure.
    • A 30-day grace period to recover anything before it’s permanently deleted.
